Our Journey

The Story Behind Elite Design

Founded in 2008 by renowned designer Sarah Mitchell, Elite Interior Design Studio began as a small boutique firm with a big vision: to create spaces that don't just look beautiful, but feel like home.

2008

The Beginning

Sarah Mitchell opens Elite Design Studio with a focus on residential interiors in downtown.

2012

Expansion

Added commercial design services and expanded team to include specialized consultants.

2016

Recognition

Won our first "Interior Design Firm of the Year" award and opened second location.

2020

Innovation

Launched virtual design services and sustainable design initiatives.

2024

Today

Leading the industry with 500+ completed projects and expanding internationally.
